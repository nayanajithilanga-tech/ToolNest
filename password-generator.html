Why use a strong password?

A strong password is your first line of defense against cyberattacks. Using long, complex passwords that mix different character types makes it computationally difficult for attackers to guess or brute-force your accounts.

Best Practices

  • Never reuse passwords across different sites.
  • Use a minimum of 12 characters, ideally 16 or more.
  • Utilize a password manager to store them securely.
